Study Design Overview

Effects of an AI-Powered Game-Based Rehabilitation Intervention
on Manual Dexterity Impairment in Neurodevelopmental Disorders

Broaden the diagnoses, but anchor strongly on a single functional criterion — manual dexterity impairment.

Inclusion / Exclusion

Inclusion

  • Age 4–8 years
  • Developmental / neurodevelopmental diagnosis
  • Confirmed manual dexterity impairment
  • Understands simple instructions
  • Plays a 10–15 min game with caregiver
  • Home use ≥ 5×/week feasible

Exclusion

  • Uncontrolled seizures
  • Severe visual / auditory impairment
  • Severe behavioral issues (block gameplay)
  • Recent botox / surgery / intensive UL therapy
  • Cognitive/behavioral level precluding assessment
